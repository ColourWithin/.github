# Colour Within

We make a colouring app for iPad that respects your attention.

## What we're building

**Colour Within** is a meditative, offline-first colouring experience: you pick a vector page, choose colours from a working set, and fill regions with pencil, marker, and tap-fill tools. Strokes stay clipped to the region boundaries. The matching regions glow softly. There are no streaks, no daily quests, no notifications nagging you back.

We think colouring is something you do *to* yourself, not something an app does *to* you.

## How we work

A small set of values we try to keep:

- **Honest defaults.** The app's posture out of the box is the one most users would consciously choose if asked.
- **Offline-first.** Your drawings stay on your iPad. There is no cloud sync, no account, no shared library.
- **Privacy as a contract, not a footnote.** Our `PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy` is auditable, our App Store Connect questionnaire mirrors it row-for-row, and the audit is checked into the repo.
- **No engagement traps.** We don't optimise for time-on-app. We optimise for the experience of finishing a page and putting the iPad down.
